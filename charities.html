A portion of the proceeds from these auctions are being donated to charities across the world.

  • Florence (JA) - works for children with disabilities and against child poverty & abuse
  • Japanese Red Cross (EN, JA)
  • World Food Program (EN, JA)
  • Ashinaga (EN, JA) - works to support children who have lost one or both of their parents and for their education
  • Japan Heart (EN, JA) - delivers healthcare to medically isolated areas
